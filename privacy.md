## Analytics

This website uses **Google Analytics** to collect anonymised data about how visitors interact with the site, including pages visited, time spent on site, and browser type. This information is used solely to improve the website experience.

**What we collect:**
- Pages visited and navigation paths
- Approximate geographic location (country/region level only)
- Browser type, device type, and operating system
- Time and duration of visits

**What we do not collect:**
- Personally identifiable information (name, email, IP address)
- Any data that can identify you as an individual

## Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique users and sessions. These are small text files stored in your browser.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}, or by disabling cookies in your browser settings.

For more information on how Google processes this data, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
