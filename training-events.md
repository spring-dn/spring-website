<p>
SPRING network-wide activities includes specialized schools, workshops, applied research seminars, as well as a
final workshop and a final conference, that will build the framework for an interdisciplinary and comprehensive PhD
training program to educate the next generation of top-notch European resilience researchers and experts. 
</p>

    <li> Transferable skills training series</li>

</ul>
